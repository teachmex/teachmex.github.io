Features That
Can be Available By Everyone

School or collage text-books are organized in tree structure of concepts. Our Learning materials are organized in a gient graph and tree data structure where connections are made through hierarchical relationship of contents, logics, basic concepts and learners average learning patterns. Every learner's learning trajectory is recorded, analized and recomendation is made while achievemnt is coded into a array of scores. Let the learning redefined!

I-Initial Assessments

An initial assessment is conducted on every learner's understanding of subject matter. The initial state is declaired through initial assessment. As learner start to learn the concepts, he/she creates a personalized learning trajectory over the vast knowledge-space. As learner propagates into the vast network of concepts, learner's knowledge status is updated.

II-Learning Trajectory

Every learner is like a random walker visiting several nodes in the vast knowledge-space. In each node, learner learns a specific concept (e.g., what is qubit in one node and representing qubit in bloch sphere in another node). After finishing the content, learner is asked a specific quiz or problem to measure their accomplishment. When learner visits 50-100 nodes, we prepare a learner's learning trajectory and concepts visited. After accomplishing sufficient amount of nodes in a specific topics (e.g., algorithms and data-structure), a learner prepares a fingure-printing of own learning attitude, understanding concepts as apersonalized scores (tensor).

III-Progress Reports

When learner learns sufficient amount of concepts by visiting many concept nodes, we can simultaneously keep updating learner's progress score in an array highlighting progress in basic math, logic, coding, theory etc. Progress report not only measures the learner's ability to solve the problem, it will also measure the efficiency, exploratory attitude, problem solving strategy development skills etc. Progress report is not created at the end, it is created side by side as a learner propagates into the knowledge-space. It appears like hexagons in the hive is filled with honey day by day!

IV-Special Recomendation

As learner start to build up learning history, based on their learning ability, our AI basd system activates the different level of exploration (e.g., accessing knowledge-space in research and articles in arxive organized in graph data structure). On completing one concept, another higher concept in the hirarchy is recomended if required fundamental concepts are done. Recomendation can lead to research level as well. For example a curious explorer can learn and explore the concepts from recently added arxive contents.

V-Basic Concept Detours

When a learner feels lacking in basic concepts (e.g., math, coding) befor heading to learn the provided concept (e.g., product of matrices not understood while taking machine learning, a detour is recomended to a algebra brushup). Detour is recomended automatically when learner is failed to answer or respond interactively in the learning platform. Learner can switch to basic concepts directly through links provided and comback based on their requirement.

V-Additional Exploration and Research

A true structure of a concept or knowledge is a graph and network (e.g., knowledge-graph), not a text-book or a couple of pdf files. Cutting edge computational tools can enhance our learning and exploring exercise. Since our platform is personalized for a learner, it can also guide a researcher to explore vast amount of knowledge space e.g., arxive papers in Quantum Computing.
